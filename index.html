About Me

Hello, I'm Edgar!

Passionate about engineering and coding. I graduated from the University of Indonesia with a degree in Electrical Engineering and further honed my skills through the Hacktive8 Software Engineering Bootcamp. With over 2 years of experience in IT, I've worked on developing and optimizing solutions that improve customer care and streamline processes. I'm always excited to learn and grow, applying my knowledge to create innovative and efficient systems.

My goal in life is to bring solutions through my engineering and programming prowess. I am driven by the opportunity to make a meaningful impact on the world through the application of technology, solving problems, and creating value for both individuals and organizations.
